Landscape Design

Give your garden a new view

Our highly qualified in-house design team will provide tailored, landscape design solutions that match your specific needs, and the architecture of your home in conjunction with the structural constraints and geology of your site. All these issues are carefully evaluated during the initial site visit and consultation, and then followed up with a detailed site plan, design strategy and itemised estimate. These designs will provide you with a complete, carefully documented garden solution, which will also cover any necessary council requirements. This bespoke document will detail all aspects of your project, including material selection, planting options for all nominated trees and plants, soil conditioning, irrigation and lighting.
